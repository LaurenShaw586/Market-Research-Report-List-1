<p><p>Thyristor modules are classified into three main market types based on voltage levels: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage modules typically handle applications under 1 kV, suitable for consumer electronics and industrial controls. Medium voltage modules operate within the 1 kV to 35 kV range, often used in motor drives and power distribution. High voltage modules exceed 35 kV, essential for large-scale utilities, renewable energy systems, and industrial applications requiring substantial power handling and control.</p></p>
