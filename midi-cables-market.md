<p><p>The MIDI cables market comprises two primary types: single cables and multicore cables. Single cables are designed for individual MIDI connections, providing a straightforward solution for connecting instruments or devices with minimal interference. In contrast, multicore cables bundle multiple MIDI channels into a single cable, facilitating more complex setups often found in studios or live performances. Multicore options offer streamlined connectivity and reduced cable clutter, catering to professional needs where multiple devices need to be linked efficiently.</p></p>
