<p><p>The short-range servo motors market includes 2-phase and 3-phase hybrid stepper motors, both offering precision control in various applications. 2-phase hybrid stepper motors utilize two coils and provide simpler, more cost-effective solutions for low to moderate torque requirements. In contrast, 3-phase hybrid stepper motors employ three coils, delivering higher torque and smoother operation, ideal for demanding applications. Both types are widely used in automation, robotics, and CNC machinery, enhancing efficiency and performance in short-range positioning tasks.</p></p>
